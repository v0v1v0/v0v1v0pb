moneyDuration R Documentation

Calculates Money Duration of a Bond.

Description

Calculates Money Duration of a Bond.

Usage

moneyDuration(macDuration, ytm, pvFullBondPrice)

Arguments

macDuration

A number.
ytm

A number.
pvFullBondPrice

A number.

Details

According to information provided by Adams and Smith (2019), the method moneyDuration() is developed to calculate Money Duration of a Bond.

Value

Input values to three arguments macDuration,ytm and pvFullBondPrice.
