NFL2010 R Documentation

The 2010 NFL Regular Season

Description

A dataframe containing all match results with 5 columns

Usage

NFL2010

Format

A dataframe containing all match results with 5 columns

home.team

Team who plays at home

away.team

Team who plays away

home.win

Take "1" if home team wins

away.win

Take "1" if away team wins

date

Number of days until now
