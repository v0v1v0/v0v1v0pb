Description

Data including environmental measurements around Bordeaux from CANDHIS database and data from InfoClimat website. CANDHIS national in situ wave measurement database. The measurements were carried out within the framework of a collaboration between the Grand Port Maritime de Nantes St-Nazaire, the Ã‰cole Centrale de Nantes and CEREMA. In addition, data from the InfoClimat site over the same period are used.
