baymedr: Computation of Bayes Factors for Common Biomedical Designs

Description

baymedr provides functions for the computation of Bayes factors for common biomedical research designs.

Details

Package: baymedr
Type: Package
Version: 0.1.1
License: GPL-3
Date: 2021-03-26

At this point in time, baymedr entails tests for the following research designs with a continuous outcome measure:

  • Equivalence: equiv_bf

  • Non-Inferiority: infer_bf

  • Superiority: super_bf
