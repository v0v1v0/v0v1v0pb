salary R Documentation

Salaries of 459 US statistics professor data

Description

A university union collected data on the salaries of 459 US statistics professors and the number of years for which they had been professors during the period from 1980 to 1990 (Bailar, 1991).

Usage

data("salary")

Format

A data frame with 459 observations on the following 2 variables.

Salaries

Salaries of 459 US statistics professor (1000s of dollars).

Years

Number of years as professor

Source

[1] Bailar, B. (1991) Salary survey of U.S. colleges and universities offering degrees in statistics. Amstat News, 182, 3-10.

References

[1] Bailar, B. (1991) Salary survey of U.S. colleges and universities offering degrees in statistics. Amstat News, 182, 3-10.
